About Our School

Freehold Township High School hold Township High School is one of six schools in the Freehold Regional High School District.

Accredited by the Middle States Association of Colleges and Schools and the New Jersey Department of Education, the Patriot school community was created in 1972. Situated on 110 acres in western Monmouth County, the comprehensive academic program provides educational opportunities for all in accordance with individual needs, interests, and abilities. School enrollment is approximated at 2,044 for the year.
